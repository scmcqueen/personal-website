Skyeler C. McQueen

Personal Information

Skyeler is passionate about math education. While at Cornell, she volunteered at the Math Explorer's Club to teach middle and high school students about advanced topics in math and at the Julia Robinson Festival to stimulate young womens' interest in STEM. She also was on the executive board of the Association for Women in Mathematics Cornell Chapter, where she would host article discussions and social events on a biweekly basis.

Source: Emily Dautschend

Some of Skyeler's other hobbies include hiking, swimming, snorkeling, and crocheting.
