Häkeln Himmel

Welcome to Häkeln Himmel! My name is Skye (or Himmel in German) and I've been crocheting (häkeln) for about 15 years. My mom taught me to crochet when I was 7 years old and I went from making fuzzy Hannah Montana-esque scarves to sweaters and amigurumi, or crocheted stuffed animals. During college, crocheting has functioned as my stress-reliever. I went from crocheting every once in a while, to every night! The more I crocheted, the more I improved and evolved. I'd like to share my progress and the patterns I've designed with my fellow avid crocheters and anyone interested in the art of häkeln!

On this website you will find a catalogue of my past crochet projects, updates on my current crochet projects, and links to the patterns.
